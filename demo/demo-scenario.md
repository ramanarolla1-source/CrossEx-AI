# CrossEx AI Demo Scenario

## Purpose

This document describes the demonstration scenario used in the CrossEx AI presentation and demo video.

The objective is to illustrate how CrossEx AI transforms witness statements into structured cross-examination preparation.

---

# Scenario

## Case Context

A witness provides a statement regarding a road traffic accident.

During the statement, the witness makes multiple assertions regarding arrival time and observations at the accident scene.

CrossEx AI is used to:

* Extract facts
* Build a timeline
* Identify inconsistencies
* Generate cross-examination questions

---

# Sample Witness Statement

I was driving home on the evening of May 10.

I arrived at the intersection at approximately 8:00 PM.

I noticed a collision involving two vehicles.

I remained at the scene for several minutes and spoke to one of the drivers.

Later, I recalled reaching the location around 7:45 PM.

I observed one vehicle crossing the signal before the collision occurred.

---

# Step 1 – Fact Extraction

CrossEx AI extracts the material facts.

## Extracted Facts

* Date: May 10
* Location: Intersection
* Arrival Time: 8:00 PM
* Arrival Time: 7:45 PM
* Observation: Two-vehicle collision
* Observation: One vehicle crossed the signal
* Action: Witness spoke to one driver

---

# Step 2 – Timeline Analysis

CrossEx AI organizes the facts chronologically.

## Timeline

| Event                      | Time            |
| -------------------------- | --------------- |
| Witness reaches location   | 7:45 PM         |
| Witness reaches location   | 8:00 PM         |
| Collision observed         | After arrival   |
| Witness speaks with driver | After collision |

Potential issue identified:

Two different arrival times are present in the witness narrative.

---

# Step 3 – Contradiction Detection

CrossEx AI reviews the statement for inconsistencies.

## Detected Issue

### Timeline Inconsistency

Statement A:

"I arrived at the intersection at approximately 8:00 PM."

Statement B:

"I recalled reaching the location around 7:45 PM."

Observation:

The witness provides two different arrival times.

This inconsistency may require clarification during cross-examination.

---

# Step 4 – Question Generation

CrossEx AI generates reviewable cross-examination questions.

## Generated Questions

### Fact Confirmation

Can you confirm the exact time you arrived at the intersection?

### Timeline Verification

What occurred between your arrival and your observation of the collision?

### Contradiction-Focused Question

You previously stated that you arrived at the intersection at approximately 8:00 PM. Why did you later state that you reached the location around 7:45 PM?

### Credibility Testing

How confident are you regarding the timeline of events described in your statement?

---

# Step 5 – Lawyer Review

CrossEx AI presents the generated questions for lawyer review.

The lawyer may:

* Approve questions
* Edit questions
* Remove questions
* Add additional questions

No question is considered final until reviewed and approved.

---

# Expected Output

The final output is a structured cross-examination preparation package containing:

* Extracted Facts
* Timeline Analysis
* Contradiction Review
* Suggested Questions
* Lawyer-Approved Question Set

This demonstration was selected because it clearly illustrates the core value of CrossEx AI:

Transforming unstructured witness narratives into structured questioning opportunities.

* Witness Statements
