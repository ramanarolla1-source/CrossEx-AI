# CrossEx AI

## From Witness Statements to Cross-Examination Questions in Minutes

### AI-Native Litigation Workflow for Lawyers

CrossEx AI is an Anna-native litigation workflow that helps lawyers transform witness statements into structured cross-examination preparation.

Instead of acting as a generic legal chatbot, CrossEx AI extracts key facts, identifies contradictions, generates targeted cross-examination questions, and keeps lawyers fully in control through a mandatory review process.

---

## Problem

Cross-examination preparation remains one of the most time-consuming tasks in litigation.

Lawyers spend hours reviewing:

* Witness Statements
* Affidavits
* Complaints
* Depositions

to identify:

* Contradictions
* Omissions
* Credibility Issues
* Questioning Opportunities

Traditional AI tools stop at document summarization. Litigation preparation requires structured analysis, contradiction detection, and reviewable question generation.

---

## Solution

CrossEx AI transforms witness statements into litigation-ready questioning strategies through a structured workflow.

```text
Witness Statement
        ↓
Fact Extraction
        ↓
Timeline Analysis
        ↓
Contradiction Detection
        ↓
Question Generation
        ↓
Lawyer Review
        ↓
Export
```

The goal is simple:

**Analyze Faster. Question Smarter. Litigate Better.**

---

## Key Features

### Fact Extraction

Automatically extracts material facts and witness assertions.

### Timeline Analysis

Builds a structured sequence of events from witness narratives.

### Contradiction Detection

Identifies inconsistencies, omissions, and credibility concerns.

### Cross-Examination Builder

Generates targeted questioning strategies based on extracted facts.

### Lawyer-in-the-Loop Review

All generated questions remain editable and reviewable before use.

### Exportable Work Product

Generated question sets can be exported for trial preparation.

---

## Example Workflow

### Witness Statement

"I arrived at the intersection at 8 PM."

Later in the statement:

"I reached the location around 7:45 PM."

### CrossEx AI Analysis

Detected Timeline Inconsistency:

* Arrival Time: 8:00 PM
* Arrival Time: 7:45 PM

Generated Question:

"You previously stated that you arrived at the intersection at 8 PM. Why did you later state that you reached the location around 7:45 PM?"
