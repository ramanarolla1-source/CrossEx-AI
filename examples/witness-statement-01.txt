# Witness Statement 01

Case Reference: Road Traffic Accident

I am employed as a sales executive and was returning home from work on the evening of May 10.

I was driving northbound on Main Road and approached the Central Junction intersection.

I arrived at the intersection at approximately 8:00 PM.

As I approached the junction, I observed two vehicles collide near the traffic signal.

I stopped my vehicle and remained at the scene for several minutes.

I spoke briefly with the driver of a white sedan involved in the collision.

I later recalled reaching the location around 7:45 PM because I remember checking my phone shortly before the accident.

I observed emergency responders arrive approximately ten minutes after the collision.

To the best of my knowledge, the above statement is true and correct.
