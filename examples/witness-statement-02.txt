# Witness Statement 02

Case Reference: Parking Lot Altercation

I am a shop owner and was present near the parking area behind Market Plaza on June 2.

At approximately 6:30 PM, I noticed two individuals arguing near a parked vehicle.

The area was well lit and I had a clear view of both individuals from a distance of approximately fifteen meters.

I observed the taller individual push the other person before leaving the area.

I remained at the location for several minutes and later provided information to the police.

When first questioned, I stated that I clearly observed the entire incident.

However, after reviewing the events, I now recall that a delivery truck briefly blocked my view during part of the altercation.

I cannot say with certainty whether physical contact occurred before or after the truck passed through the area.

I believe the above statement is accurate to the best of my recollection.
