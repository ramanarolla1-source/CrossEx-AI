# Cross-Examination Questions

## Fact Confirmation Questions

1. Can you confirm the exact time you arrived at the intersection?

2. How did you determine the time of your arrival?

3. Did you check a watch, phone, or other device at the time?

---

## Timeline Verification Questions

4. What occurred immediately after you arrived at the intersection?

5. How much time passed between your arrival and your observation of the collision?

6. Did any other significant events occur before the collision?

---

## Contradiction-Focused Questions

7. You previously stated that you arrived at the intersection at approximately 8:00 PM. Why did you later state that you reached the location around 7:45 PM?

8. Which of the two arrival times do you believe is accurate?

9. What explains the difference between the two statements?

## Credibility Testing Questions

10. How confident are you in your recollection of the timeline?

11. Have you discussed the events with anyone before providing this statement?

12. Did you review any documents or reports before making this statement?
