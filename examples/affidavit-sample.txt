# Affidavit Sample

AFFIDAVIT

I, Rajesh Kumar, aged 42 years, residing at 15 Green Avenue, Hyderabad, do hereby solemnly affirm and state as follows:

1. I am the deponent herein and I am well acquainted with the facts and circumstances of the present matter.

2. On March 14, 2026, at approximately 9:00 AM, I arrived at the commercial premises situated at Plot No. 18, Industrial Estate, Hyderabad.

3. Upon arrival, I observed that the main entrance to the premises was locked and no employees were present at the location.

4. I remained at the premises for approximately thirty minutes and attempted to contact the site manager.

5. During this period, I did not observe any business activity being conducted at the premises.

6. At approximately 9:20 AM, I observed two individuals exiting the building through a side entrance.

7. I subsequently left the location at approximately 9:25 AM.

8. I state that the premises remained closed throughout my visit and no persons entered or exited the building during that time.

9. I later submitted a written complaint regarding the condition and operation of the premises.

10. The facts stated herein are true and correct to the best of my knowledge and belief.

DEPONENT

VERIFICATION

I, Rajesh Kumar, the above-named deponent, do hereby verify that the contents of this affidavit are true and correct to the best of my knowledge and belief and that nothing material has been concealed therefrom.

Verified at Hyderabad on this 15th day of March, 2026.

DEPONENT
